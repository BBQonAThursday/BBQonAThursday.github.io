Iceland

Getaway Destination in May 2021

Earn Your Trip with 2020 Sales

Preparations are underway for the 2021 trip to exotic, extraordinary Iceland. Don’t let the country’s name fool you—Iceland touts a lush, green, and diverse landscape. From colorful mountains to cascading waterfalls to glistening glaciers, this getaway location offers impressive natural attractions that are jaw-droppingly beautiful in person. And because volcanic springs reside next to glaciers, Iceland is poetically called the land of fire and ice—it’s a natural contrast not seen anywhere else in the world.

Stay tuned as we refine our itinerary! But know that exhilarating excursions and distinctive dining are always on the Graber Getaways agenda.

Qualification period is December 29, 2019 – January 2, 2021.

Golden Circle Tour

Travel the Golden Circle, the legendary sightseeing route that features Þingvallavatn National Park, the Geysir Geothermal Area, and Gullfoss Waterfall. The sheer grandeur cannot be overstated. Plus, there are lots of interesting detours just off the beaten path.

Blue Lagoon

Iceland touts many natural hot springs, including the renowned Blue Lagoon. At 102°F, the spa-like waters soothe the body and the soul.

South Coast Tour

This tour showcases the diverse natural features of Iceland’s south coast. Walk on a glacier. Stroll a lava field. Admire the many magnificent waterfalls. Then head to Vik’s stunning coastline and its black sand beaches.

Whale Watching Tour

Ready your camera to capture the majestic giants of the ocean. What a thrilling sight to see! Get up close and personal with the whales and other wildlife, like dolphins, porpoises, and puffins.

Hilton Reykjavic Noridca

The stylish Hilton Reykjavic Noridca Hotel is the homebase for our Graber Getaways trip to Iceland.
