Two-Weapon Fighting

When you engage in two-weapon fighting, you can add your ability modifier to the damage of the second attack.

Second Wind

You have a limited well of stamina that you can draw on to protect yourself from harm. On your turn, you can use a bonus action to regain hit points equal to 1d10 + your fighter level. Once you use this feature, you must finish a short or long rest before you can use it again.

Action Surge

Starting at 2nd level, you can push yourself beyond your normal limits for a moment. On your turn, you can take one additional action on top of your regular action and a possible bonus action.

Once you use this feature, you must finish a short or long rest before you can use it again. Starting at 17th level, you can use it twice before a rest, but only once on the same turn.

Protection

When a creature you can see attacks a target other than you within 15ft. of you, you can use your reaction to intercept the attack and become the new target. Your ending location is between the attacker and targeted creature.

Dual Shielding

At level 3 when you choose this archetype, you gain the Protection and Two-Weapon fighting fighting styles. If you hold a shield in your main hand and a shield in your offhand, you gain a +1 bonus to your AC on top of the normal bonus of a shield.Some of your shield talents require your target to make a saving through to resist.

Offensive Defense

At level 3, your shield(s) are now considered light strength-base melle weapons, dealing 1d6 Bludgeoning damage and with the throw property (20/40). If the shield has any magical properties it is treated as a magical weapon. Any bonus's the shield grants are also treated as bonuess to attack and damage rolls.

Shield Talents

More to come as I earn them.

3rd Level Shield Talent

Shield Defense: As a bonus action, you can enter a defensive stance that lasts until the start of your next turn. While in your defensive stance, you can make opportunity attacks without using your reaction, and you can use your reaction to make a melee attack against a creature that moves more than 5t while within your reach.

Extra Attack

Beginning at 5th level, you can attack twice, instead of once, whenever you take the Attack action on your turn.

The number of attacks increases to three when you reach 11th level in this class and to four when you reach 20th level in this class.
