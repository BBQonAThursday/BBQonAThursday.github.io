Hey! That's me up there ^!!! Based out of Madison, WI, I currently work at Springs Window Fashions as a Web Designer but my role falls more along the lines of performing web maintainence and Web Designer / Front-End Developer. I've been here for roughly 2 years now and have had the opportunity to work on roughly 5 different site set-ups and work on many different web updates, issues, page builds and maintainence workflows.
